About Me

Hi, I'm Pato, a passionate pixel artist and indie game developer with over 8 years of experience creating digital art and ( mainly ) games.

I have always liked videogames and the digital world, and one lucky day I decided to watch a full 12 hour tutorial on C# and Unity. Since then, my abilities have only gotten better and my drive to get better and reach further is even higher.

When I'm not experimenting with game mechanics, you can find me drawing pixels, studying character designm, in one of my 1235 side projects, or drinking way too much coffee.
